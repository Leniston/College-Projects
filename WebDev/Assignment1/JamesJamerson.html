James Jamerson

James Jamerson, a true legend of Bass playing. He revolutionized the sound of Motown Records in the 1960s with his innovative and melodic bass lines. They became the backbone of countless Motown hits. You can find his work on tracks by many famous artists such as, Marvin Gaye, Stevie Wonder, The Jackson 5 and Diana Ross.

Sadly clips of Jamerson are rare, but this cover of his work on The Jackson 5 track 'Dear Darling' gives a taste of the sort of Bass lines he laid down.
