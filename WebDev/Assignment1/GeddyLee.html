Geddy Lee

Rush is a band known for its cult following, 20 minute long instrumental songs and powerful sound. Their bassist is a Canadian rock legend, but he isn't just the frontman, he's their Bassist, keyboardist and vocalist too! Known for his soaring vocals, intricate and aggressive bass lines, and impressive keyboard skills, Geddy has been a driving force behind Rush's success for decades.
