Joe Dart

Joe Dart is a virtuoso Bass from Harbor Springs, Michigan in the USA. He is a pivotal member of the renowned funk band Vulfpeck. Dart has solidified his position among some of the greatest Bassists with his fast, melodic and catchy bass lines that are often at the front of Vulfpeck's music.

But don't take my word, have a listen for yourself...
