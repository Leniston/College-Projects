Frequently Asked Questions

To ensure the safety and health of all our guests, we require pets to be up-to-date on their vaccinations.

For Dogs:

  • Rabies
  • Distemper
  • Parvovirus
  • Bordetella (kennel cough)

For Cats:

  • Rabies
  • FVRCP (Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis, Calicivirus, and Panleukopenia)

Please bring a copy of your pet's vaccination records when you drop them off.

We want your pet to feel as comfortable as possible during their time with us. You can bring:

  • Their favorite bed or blanket (optional but recommended)
  • A supply of their regular food to avoid dietary changes
  • Any medication they may need with clear instructions
  • A leash or carrier for safe transportation
